Ratatouille


Description

Ratatouille is a healthy and easy-to-digest stew of summer vegetables. A classic ratatouille includes eggplant (aubergines), zucchini (courgettes), bell peppers, tomatoes, onions, garlic, olive oil and herbs. Its name is a combination of the French word “rata” meaning a stew with whole pieces and the verb “touiller” which means to stir, toss, or mix.

Ingredients Required

  • Garlic and oil
  • An eggplant
  • 2 zucchini
  • 2 tomatoes
  • fresh mushrooms
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 red or green bell pepper.
  • dried parsley and salt.
  • grated Parmesan cheese

How to Make Ratatouille

  1. In a skillet, cook the garlic in olive oil. Add the eggplant, parsley, and salt.
  2. Cook until the eggplant is tender, then spread the mixture in a prepared casserole dish and sprinkle with Parmesan
  3. Spread the zucchini on top and sprinkle with more cheese. Continue layering according to the instructions in Step 3.
  4. Bake the ratatouille in the preheated oven until the vegetables are tender.
